Today we live in times where we are flooded by social media. To go against this wave, we have to go back to the simple things in life. The great adventure of my life is always looking for a form to deal with the unclear amount of things that sneak into our 'being'. Emotions of all kinds fed by the fleeting, fast world that never seems to end. Unless we examine our feelings, thoughts, opinions, experiences, choices, daily developments with which we are confronted with a magnifying glass. This vastly enlarged view of myself and the world is a form that makes me more independent, stronger, richer and bigger as a person. Just like everyone else in this world. We can not go for less than this.
